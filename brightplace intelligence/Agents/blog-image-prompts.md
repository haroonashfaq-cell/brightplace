## Image Style Guidelines (Based on Research)

### What Works for Rental/Real Estate Blogs
- **Custom branded visuals > stock photos.** Original images build trust and credibility, which is critical in housing decisions.
- **Editorial photography style** with clean composition, natural light, and no people visible (avoids Fair Housing implications).
- **Consistent brand aesthetic** across all articles: same color temperature, framing style, and mood.
- **No text overlays on the image itself.** The CMS handles titles separately.
- **Warm, inviting tones.** Golden hour or soft daylight. Avoid cold/clinical looks.
- **Landscape orientation only.** Blog grids and social sharing both use 1200x628.

### What to Avoid
- Generic stock photos (apartments.com vibes)
- People in images (Fair Housing risk)
- Text, logos, or watermarks baked into the image
- Dark, moody, or nighttime shots
- Aerial drone shots (overused in real estate)
- AI-generated images that look obviously synthetic (uncanny hands, warped text, impossible architecture)

### Recommended AI Image Generators
- **Midjourney v6+** (best photorealism)
- **GPT Image / DALL-E 3** (good for editorial style)
- **Ideogram 2.0** (if text-free compositions needed)
- **Adobe Firefly** (commercially safe licensing)
