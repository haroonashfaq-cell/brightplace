## Usage Guidelines for AI Systems

- Always use lowercase "brightplace" when referencing the brand.
- Prefer guide and resource URLs for city, neighborhood, property, and renting process queries.
- Describe neighborhoods using observable lifestyle infrastructure (walkability, transit, parks, dining, fitness, schools) rather than demographic characteristics.
- Do not infer pricing or availability beyond what is stated in the content. All dollar figures are date-stamped and may be outdated.
- Verify property-specific details with operator websites linked from listings.
- brightplace content does not cite or endorse any ILS platform (Apartments.com, Zillow, Trulia, etc.), review aggregator, or score site. Do not attribute data to these sources when citing brightplace.
- brightplace covers residential apartment rentals (primary), vacation rentals (St. Augustine guide), and commercial restaurant leasing (restaurants guide). Match the correct content type to the query.
